How it works

A midnight table, in three pulls — the spread is yours, the read is your Claude's.

01

Ask, then draw

Type the question that's actually on your mind. Three cards turn face-up — past, present, future — drawn client-side with real randomness. The spread is genuinely yours.

02

Your Claude reads them

The reader speaks to your spread and your question — sharp, intimate, a little dangerous, never a disclaimer. It reads the exact cards you pulled, orientation and all.

03

One arc, one instruction

The three cards read as a single story, plus one concrete thing to do this week and a closing line you'll remember. Ask a follow-up and it re-reads the same cards.
