How it works

A blunt chart in three steps — no ephemeris, no server, no mysticism.

01

Give it your birth data

Date, time, and place — or open a sample soul. If Switchboard already knows you, the intake is prefilled and it reads the moment you connect.

02

Your Claude reads the sky

No tools, no backend: your own model estimates the placements and delivers verdicts, not horoscopes. Nothing about your birth ever leaves the tab.

03

Sun, moon, rising, a full ephemeris, do's and don'ts, and one line about today. Come back tomorrow and the brief re-reads itself — the chart doesn't move.
