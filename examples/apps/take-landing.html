How it works

Script, then shoot — one line in, a finished take on your drive.

01

Say what you're recording

One line: a demo, a pitch, an update. Pick screen-and-mic or camera-and-mic, and lend a context if you want the script grounded in it.

02

Your Claude writes the beats

Three script angles, one recommended. Each beat is what's on screen and the line you say over it. Steer it plainer, punchier, or shorter to redraft.

03

Record in the page, keep the file

Hit record against the script, capture locally, download the .webm. Claude only ever sees your one line — never the recording itself.

What you get

A script to hit and a recorder to hit it in — side by side, one take.
