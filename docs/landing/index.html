How it works

A sidekick on your machine. A provider in every page.

Three parts, one trust chain — and the page never touches your keys.

01 · THE SIDEKICK

A daemon holds your AI

A tiny local app keeps your Claude sign-in, your MCP tools, and your context. It's the only thing that ever sees a credential — and it runs on loopback, paired to your browser by a token.

02 · THE PROVIDER

The extension injects window.claude

Every page gets one standard provider — like window.ethereum, but the asset is your model, tools, and context. The page asks; it never holds a key.

03 · THE GATE

Every action is brokered

Reads auto-approve within scope. Writes raise an out-of-band consent card you click. Nothing bypasses it — not the page, not the model. You can revoke any app, any time.

Your computer is the backend now.

Install the sidekick, add the extension, and every compatible site runs on the AI you already pay for. Free and open source.
